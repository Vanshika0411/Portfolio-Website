Project Overview-:

To develop an interactive quiz application that tests user's knowledge of Data Analytics concepts, tools, and techniques. The application will include various types of questions to provide a comprehensive assessment and engaging experience. This Quiz contains 10 questions. These questions are based on the Data Science and Big Data Analytics. Each question contain of total 4 options in which you have to choose the 1 corret answer. Each question will be of 1 mark. No negative marking will be allowed. And in the end, you will also know your score, how many you got right and how many you got wrong questions in the Quiz...
